Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my Trajectory Fitness+ subscription?
Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name, tap Subscriptions, select Trajectory Fitness+, and tap Cancel Subscription.

How do I restore a purchase after reinstalling the app?
Open the paywall screen in the app and tap "Restore Purchases."
