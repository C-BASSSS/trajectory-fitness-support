Trajectory Fitness Privacy Policy

Effective July 20, 2026

Trajectory Fitness ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it's used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

Photos you choose to analyze (current physique, goal physique, and progress check-in photos) and the training profile you enter (age, sex, height, weight, training frequency, experience, diet adherence, equipment) are sent to our backend server and then to our AI provider, Anthropic, solely to generate your physique analysis, timeline estimate, macro targets, and workout plan. We do not store your photos or profile data on our servers after an analysis completes -- each request is processed and discarded.

Your name and birthdate are stored only on your device and are never transmitted anywhere. Your plan, settings, streak, daily tracking log, and progress check-in history are stored only on your device and are never uploaded to any server we control.

If you subscribe to Trajectory Fitness+, your purchase is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. To verify an active subscription, our backend receives a cryptographically signed proof of purchase from your device via Apple's StoreKit -- this identifies your subscription status, not your payment information.

2. How We Use Information

Photos and profile data are used solely to generate the analysis, plan, and estimates you requested, at the moment you request them. We do not use your data for advertising, do not sell your data, and do not share it with third parties beyond Anthropic (to perform the analysis you requested) and Apple (to process subscription purchases).

3. Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic (Claude): processes the photos and profile text you submit to generate your analysis. Their own privacy policy governs how they handle that data.
  • Apple (StoreKit, and local notifications): manages subscription purchases and delivers the reminder notifications you enable in Settings.

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

4. Your Choices and Data Control

Everything Trajectory Fitness stores is on your device. You can delete your plan and tracking history at any time from Settings, or delete everything by uninstalling the app. You can turn off notifications at any time in iOS Settings. To ask about or request deletion of any data that may briefly exist in server logs during processing, contact us at trajectoryfitnesssupport@gmail.com.

5. Children's Privacy

Trajectory Fitness is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has used the app to submit data, contact us and we will address it.

6. Data Security

Photos and profile data are transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) to our backend and to Anthropic. Because no user data is stored server-side after processing, there is no ongoing database of your personal data beyond the moment each request is handled.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app changes. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated effective date.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: trajectoryfitnesssupport@gmail.com
